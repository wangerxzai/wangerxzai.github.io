Convergence is an evolutionary process （主干）/ by which two unrelated organisms end up （定语从句）/ developing similar traits in order to succeed （非谓语动词）/ in a particular environment or fill a particular niche（介词短语）.

When non-avian dinosaurs went extinct 66 million years ago, mammals rose to fill many of their vacant niches. If humans were to disappear, it’s possible that birds, the only surviving dinosaurs, could fill our roles as the smartest and handiest land animals.

在6600万年前非翼类恐龙灭绝后，哺乳动物的出现填补了它们空缺的生态位。如果人类消失，那么鸟类，这个唯一存活下来的恐龙物种，很有可能作为最聪明及最灵巧的陆地动物填补我们的作用。

niche 壁龛，经济学上规模尚小的“缝隙市场”。文中是指生物为了获得生存资源而占据的一个特定最小单位。
