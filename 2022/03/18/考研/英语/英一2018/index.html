  • Trust is a tricky business. 信任是件很玄妙的事。

  • Then == if that is the situation

"既然那样、那样的话"，而therefore引出前述内容的逻辑结果。

  • When people place their trust in an dividual or an institution, their brains release oxytocin, a hormone that produces pleasurable feelings and triggers the herding instinct that prompts human to connect with one another.

When people place their trust in an dividual or an institution（时间状语从句）,/ their brains release oxytocin（主干）,/ a hormone （同位语 oxytocin）that produces pleasurable feelings and triggers the herding instinct/ that prompts human to connect with one another（定语从句修饰the herding instinct）.

这里表示从无到有的“激发本能”，所以用produce产生，而不是maintain保持。

  • Scientists have found that exposure to this hormone puts us in a trusting mood.

by，引出动作的施动者或达成动作的方式。of，引出动作主体。

  • Sixty todllers were each introduced to an adult tester holding a plastic container.

60名学步童被各自引导着与一位手持塑料容器的成人测试员认识。
